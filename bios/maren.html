Maren Krings

Photographer Climate Impact Storyteller


As a Climate Impact Storyteller, Maren Krings leverages her expertise in photography, filmmaking, content writing, and keynote speaking to highlight the potential of hemp for climate resilience and social justice. She holds a B.F.A. in Photography from the Savannah College of Art & Design, a degree in Climate Change Studies and Environmental Science from the University of Exeter, and multiple certifications in related fields. Krings currently serves as Executive Director for FIHO, the Federation of International Hemp Organisations.
