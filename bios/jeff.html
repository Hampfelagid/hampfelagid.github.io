Jeff Lowenfels - AK (USA)

Jeff Lowenfels is the author of the award winning quadrilogy known as the Teaming Series: Teaming With Microbes, Teaming With Bacteria, Teaming With Fungi and Teaming With Nutrients. He is an internationally known leader in the organic /sustainability movement and his books have been translated into almost a dozen languages ranging from French, Spanish and Dutch to Korean, Romanian and Turkish.
Jeff is well known for his highly regarded and entertaining lectures. Give him one hour and he will convert the world to organic cultivation using humor and science. He also pens the longest running garden column in North America, (47 years) and hosts a popular weekly podcast, “Teaming With Microbes.”
